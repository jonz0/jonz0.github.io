About me

Upbeat, competitive, and people-oriented

HELLO WORLD

My name is Jonathan. I just jumped ship!

I'm a 2021 graduate living in the San Francisco Bay Area. After graduating, I set off into business strategy and early-stage venture capital - inspired by driven founders who end up building great things. In pursuit of my own great thing, I began exploring with software development just under a year ago. I'm just keeping afloat, but I'm treading relentlessly and look forward to discovering the opportunities that lie ahead.

My work has always involved sharing information with other people, so my belief in ongoing feedback and challenging opinions is baked into my process for creating new things. I'm relatively inexperienced, but I have a passion for creating - I drop into flow most easily when I'm piecing together something tangible or interactable. It took me five years to realize this, so I'm pretty hyped to get moving.

Beyond the screen, you can find me getting my ass kicked in Brazilian jiu-jitsu, handing it right back in Super Smash Bros., or catching some waves at a beach nearby - regretting each of my 18 years living in San Diego that I didn't start surfing sooner.
