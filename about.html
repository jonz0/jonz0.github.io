About me

Upbeat, competitive, and people-oriented

HELLO WORLD

My name is Jonathan Lu. I just jumped ship!

I'm a 2021 graduate living in the San Francisco Bay Area. Right out of college, I set off into business strategy and early-stage venture capital - driven by enthusiasm for unknown founders who end up building great things. Inspired by those same founders, I'm in hot pursuit of my own great thing, so I began experimenting with software development just under a year ago. I feel like I'm barely keeping afloat, but I'm treading relentlessly, and I look forward to discovering the people and opportunities that lie ahead.

My work has always involved sharing and receiving information from other people, so my belief in ongoing feedback and challenging opinions is woven into my own process for working and iterating on projects. Although I'm a relatively inexperienced developer, I have a passion for creating useful things with my own two hands, and I comfortably drop into my flow state when I'm piecing together something that's right in front of me. It took me five years to realize this, so I'm determined to capitalize on every chance I have to improve as a creator & developer.

Outside of work, you'll find me getting my ass kicked in Brazilian jiu-jitsu, handing it right back in Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, and finally catching some green waves at a beach nearby - regretting each of my 18 years living in San Diego that I didn't start surfing sooner.
