About me

Upbeat, competitive, and people-oriented

HELLO WORLD

My name is Jonathan Lu. I just jumped ship!

I'm a 2021 graduate living in the San Francisco Bay Area. Right out of college, I set off into business strategy and early-stage venture capital - driven by my enthusiasm for unknown founders who end up building great things. In pursuit of my own great thing, I began experimenting with software development just under a year ago. I feel like I'm barely keeping afloat, but I'm treading relentlessly, and I'm eager to discover the opportunities that lie ahead.

My work has always involved sharing information with other people, so my belief in ongoing feedback and challenging opinions is woven into my process for creating new things. Although I'm relatively inexperienced, I have a passion for creating - I effortlessly drop into flow when I'm piecing together something that people like me can interact with. It took me five years to realize this, which makes me pretty hyped to get moving.

When I want a break, I'm usually getting my ass kicked in Brazilian jiu-jitsu, handing it right back in Super Smash Bros., or finally catching some green waves at a beach nearby - regretting each of my 18 years living in San Diego that I didn't start surfing sooner.
